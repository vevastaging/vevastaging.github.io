Our Platform

Our protocol takes inspiration from the prosecutor-and-jury model and game theory

VEVA is a peer-to-peer network of trustless experts. We assume analysts are either incompetent, lazy, conflicted, or corrupted and work backwards. The game theoretical mechanics eliminates bad actors/actions to provide the highest quality token analysis platform——ever.

Two analysts competing to disprove each other ensures that only the highest quality, most accurate information gets considered by the jury. Each analyst has an economic incentive to champion their side, and the jurors have similar incentives to rate the tokens accurately.

Our analysts earn 'Reputation Points' to reach new levels. Higher levels come with additional referrals, used for adding new members to the ecosystem——and the ability to earn a percentage of their income! This ensures a network of only trusted analysts.
