The Vision

VEVA aims to be the most reliable ratings platform in the world.
We believe that our game mechanics and analyst network will enable us to scale far beyond traditional systems, and we will directly disrupt legacy ratings. Our vision is simply to provide high-quality analysis without the noise.



Why are ratings desperately needed:

Blockchains, decentralized computing, and tokens are unleashing an economic transformation not seen in generations. As business processes become fully digital and global, finance is following suit. Tokens are early methods of encapsulating digital units of value for liquid global trading.

​

We see a world powered by millions of tokens traded 24/7 by algorithms. The market is decentralized and borderless, and there are no reporting, disclosure, regulation or exchange standards to support price discovery.​

​

Without any mechanism for parsing signal from noise, how do we trust prices? The promise of crypto is at risk of being hijacked by scams, pump+dump schemes, incompetent teams or manic speculation. Price discovery is a critical imperative and we hope to support this obective by provding an open database of fundamental signals to support valuation models.


Why adversarial ratings?

Decentralized computing is really trustless computing. Bitcoin / Ethereum organizes a p2p ledger assuming nodes are unreliable or corrupt; the proof-of-work protocol ensures good behavior and a robust/trusted network.

​

Similarly, VEVA is a peer-to-peer network of trustless experts. We assume analysts are either incompetent, lazy, conflicted, or corrupted and work backwards. The game theoretical mechanics eliminates bad actors/actions to provide the highest quality token analysis platform ever.

​

As VEVA grows the network maintains high and verifiable standards of quality / integrity. Many are aware of the mortgage securities rating scandals of 2008; we are committed to building a ratings platform that is conflict/collusion proof.

​

How it Works: A Typical Round

Day 1: Analysts from across the globe are randomly assigned to a token and a position as either BULL or BEAR lead, or a jury member. This randomness virtually eliminates the possibility of collusion, as nobody can control what they're arguing for or against.
Days 1-5: Bull and Bear leads do in-depth research: one trying to prove that the token is a good buy, and the other building a case for selling. These briefs will be used to sway an impartial jury, and each lead is economically incentivized to put forth the strongest argument available.
Day 6: Jury members take a valuation survey before receiving the briefs to establish a baseline, and another after reading and considering both sides of the argument. This ensures that what's measured is sway: whether or not one of the leads had an effect the general sentiment.
Day 7: VEVA tokens will be distributed according to performance, including jury members who are best able to predict the outcome. Paying losing leads and accurate jury members rewards precision and effort through game-theoretical mechanics.
After the Round: All VEVA data is available to the public at no cost via our website or API. You can browse historical data in our archives, or read our whitepaper for more information on the protocol!
