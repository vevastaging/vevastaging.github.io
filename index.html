Our Platform

VEVA is a peer-to-peer network of trustless experts, resulting in completely decentralized token ratings.
We use a prosecutor-and-jury model to extract the best arguments and facts while minimizing noise and bias.

Conflict-Proof

Quasi-random token assignments and game theoretical mechanics economically incentivize the truth.

Built on Ethereum

The blockchain guarantees the immutability of ratings logic, data, and the reward ledger.

Infinitely Scalable

VEVA is designed to be completely permissionless, allowing for incredible scalability.

Adversarial Ratings

Symmetrical opposing arguments ensure that each data point is contested and fact-checked.

How it Works: A Typical Round

Day 1: Analysts from across the globe are randomly assigned to a token and a position as either BULL or BEAR lead, or a jury member. This randomness virtually eliminates the possibility of collusion, as nobody can control what they're arguing for or against.
Days 1-5: Bull and Bear leads do in-depth research: one trying to prove that the token is a good buy, and the other building a case for selling. These briefs will be used to sway an impartial jury, and each lead is economically incentivized to put forth the strongest argument available.
Day 6: Jury members take a valuation survey before receiving the briefs to establish a baseline, and another after reading and considering both sides of the argument. This ensures that what's measured is sway: whether or not one of the leads had an effect the general sentiment.
Day 7: VEVA tokens will be distributed according to performance, including jury memebers who are best able to predict the outcome. Paying losing leads and accurate jury members rewards precision and effort through game-theoretical mechanics.
After the Round: All VEVA data is available to the public at no cost via our website or API. You can browse historical data in our archives, or read our whitepaper for more information on the protocol!
