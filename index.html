Conflict Proof

VEVA analysts are quasi-randomly assigned to tokens, and are equally as likely to be a bull as a bear. This, plus the economic incentive to win regardless of assignment, creates an environment in which the best data rises to the top.

Quality Analysts

We started with a trusted group of hand-picked experts and run a semi-closed system: nobody can serve as an analyst until they've been vetted by an existing analyst to prevent sybil attack and maintain quality.

Qualitative and Quantitative

Our system is designed to capture both strong quantative data and the kind of qualitative data that can only be extracted by humans.

Economic Inventives

Every aspect of the system has an economic incentive or disincentive, creating a robust, predictable ecosystem.

VEVA is your gateway to crypto

Trustless

VEVA is designed to be trustless. We assume analysts are either incompetent, lazy, conflicted, or corrupted and work backwards. The adversarial mechanics eliminates bad actors/actions to provide the highest quality token analysis platform ever.

Why adversarial ratings?

Decentralized computing is really trustless computing. Bitcoin/Ethereum assume nodes are unreliable or corrupt; the proof-of-work protocol ensures good behavior and a robust, trusted network.
